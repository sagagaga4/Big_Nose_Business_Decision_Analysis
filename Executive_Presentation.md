## Executive Summary

### Business Context
This analysis evaluates Big Nose, an independent music artist, to determine growth potential and strategic positioning in the music industry. The analysis covers financial performance, audience engagement, and content strategy over the past 5 years.

### Key Findings
1. **Revenue Concentration Risk**: Top 5 songs generate 83.3% of total earnings ($1,653.78 of $1,985.95)
2. **Moderate Growth Trajectory**: 4.6% follower growth in last year, with 196,561 streams
3. **Engagement Challenge**: Only 0.33% monthly active engagement rate despite 218,867 total reach
4. **Strong Content Velocity**: Average 16.7 songs per year demonstrates consistent output
5. **Assessment**: **PROMISING UPCOMING ARTIST** (Score: 6.0/10.0)

### Top 3 Recommendations
1. **Diversify Revenue Streams** - Reduce dependency on top 5 songs
2. **Convert Programmed Listeners** - Transform passive listeners into active fans
3. **Optimize Playlist Strategy** - Leverage 99 current playlist placements for growth
